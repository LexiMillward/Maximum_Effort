Welcome

Ruth's Childminding

Ruths' Childminding is a small, independent home from home childcare provider based in Tilney St Lawrence, Kings Lynn looking after children from Tilney itself and the surrounding areas. I am OFSTED registered and a qualified Early Years Teacher. I have a wealth of experience working with children in a variety of settings and roles from schools to summer play-schemes.

My main aim is to encourage and enable children to develop quickly in a fun, enriching and safe enviroment in the early years and do all i can to ensure that they have the best possible start in life.

So if you are looking for an excellent quality of care for your child in the Tilney area contact us for more information about the service we offer.

Services

Availability, Fees, Activities and Food

Availability

Ruth's childcare is open from 7:30am to 6pm Monday to Friday throughout term time and school holidays. I will be closed for all public bank holidays and between December 23rd to January 2nd.

I have 12 places available each day which breaks down into the following:

  • 1 under 1
  • 3 under 5's
  • 6 under 8's

I am aware that some days you may require an earlier start or later finish and i am willing to look at accomodating this if requested in advance.

I will try my best to accommodate your childcare needs, but I have to consider the age and requirements of the children already on role.

Over the course of each year I take 21 days holiday. I will give advance notice of at least 4 weeks so you have enough time to make alternative arrangements.

I do not charge if i am unable to work due to sickness however if your child or you are unwell and unable to attend full payment will still be required as I am unable to fill the space on such short notice.

Fees and Charges

Fees are dependant on the hours required and if you wish to include food & drink.

I provide an invoice at the beginning of each week for the following weeks bookings. Payment must be made 72 hours before your first booking of that week. Please be aware i will not provide care for your child unless payment has been made.

Charges are as follows:

  • Childcare - £4 per hour per child
  • Food - £1.50 per child
  • Nappies - 50p each (if required)
  • Cream / Wipes - 10p per use (if required)
  • Suncream - £1.50 (if required)


What is included in fees:

I will provide:

  • Food including healthy meals & snacks
  • Buggy
  • High Chair
  • Booster seat
  • Cot
  • Bibs
  • Aprons

Parents to provide:

  • Change of clothes
  • Sun cream
  • Clothing / footwear appropriate to weather
  • Nappies, cream & wipes
  • Baby formula & bottles
  • Confort toy / blanket
  • Medication
  • Food if not using my service
